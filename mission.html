Mission & Vision

What YEMC is building for Mugara's young people.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged youth by building their skills in entrepreneurship, leadership, and English language, while connecting them to opportunities.

Our Vision

To build a skilled and self-reliant generation that leads sustainable change in Mugara and helps Burundi become prosperous and connected beyond its borders.
