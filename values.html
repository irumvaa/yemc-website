Our Values

The principles that guide how YEMC learns, leads, and serves.

Perseverance

Continued effort to do or achieve something regardless of difficulties, failure, or opposition.

Innovation

Being able to come up with new ideas and projects that can benefit the future generation.

Servant Leadership

Understanding that being a leader is not about being served but instead is about serving others with commitment and passion.

Empathy

Being able to put yourself in others' shoes and understand their needs.

Integrity

The quality of being honest and having strong moral or ethical principles that you consistently adhere to, regardless of the situation.
