Our Values

The principles that guide how YEMC learns, leads, and serves.

Perseverance

Continued effort to achieve something regardless of difficulties, failure, or opposition.

Innovation

Coming up with new ideas and projects that can benefit the future generation.

Servant Leadership

Understanding that leadership is not about being served, but serving others with commitment and passion.

Empathy

Being able to put yourself in other people's shoes and understand their needs.

Integrity

Being honest and holding strong moral principles in every situation.
